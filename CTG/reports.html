Air Quality Data Use, Issues, and Value in Georgia

Published: December 2013

This case describes the air quality conditions and related programs and issues centered in the area around Atlanta, Georgia. It is part of a larger study to understand how air quality information is currently used at the community level and to assess the potential benefits of enhancing air quality monitoring data from ground sensor networks with data gathered by satellites.
