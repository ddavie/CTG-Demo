About

Nicolas is a first year graduate student studying Politcal Science at Rockefeller College of Public Affairs & Policy

What brought you to the Center for Technology in Government?

I was looking for a challenging position with the school in policy that included research and analysis. CTG offered all of that and more.

What did you do prior to coming to CTG?

I was an undergraduate in the Honors College at the University at Albany. After graduating, I worked as a social media intern for the History News Network and then a Public Relations intern for a non-profit organization (Share for Life Foundation) in NYC.
